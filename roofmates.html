Boundaries

Coordinate what's shared.
Keep context intact.

Household membership does not automatically make every expense, plan, or conversation visible to everyone. Every activity in Roofmates belongs to one of six scopes, and visibility follows participation.

Roofmates is a coordination tool and shared ledger. It does not hold or transfer roommate funds.

HouseholdVisible to every current member of the house.
EventScoped to an event's participants and invited guests.
PoolShared with the people contributing to a pooled plan.
TransactionLimited to a single expense's participants.
PrivateYours alone, or a chosen few — never house-wide by default.
GuestLets non-members join an event without joining the house.

Questions

What Roofmates
is — and is not.

Who is Roofmates for?

Adults coordinating a shared household — roommates, friends, co-living houses, and people setting up a new home together.

Does Roofmates hold or transfer money?

No. Roofmates is a coordination tool and shared ledger. It is not a bank, lender, money transmitter, or escrow service and does not custody roommate funds.

Does everyone see every activity?

No. Visibility follows participation across household, Event, Pool, transaction, private, and guest contexts.

Which platforms are planned?

Roofmates is being prepared as a native app for iOS and Android.
