Questions

What Roofmates is—and is not.

Who is Roofmates for?

Adults coordinating a shared household, including roommates, friends, co-living households, and people setting up a new home together.

Does Roofmates hold or transfer money?

No. Roofmates is a coordination tool and shared ledger. It is not a bank, lender, money transmitter, or escrow service and does not custody roommate funds.

Does everyone see every activity?

No. Roofmates distinguishes household, Event, Pool, transaction, private, and guest contexts so visibility can follow participation.

Which platforms are planned?

Roofmates is being prepared as a native app for iOS and Android.
