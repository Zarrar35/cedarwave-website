Delete your Roofmates account

Contact: zarrar@cedarwavetechnologies.com

Request deletion in the app

  1. Open Roofmates and sign in.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. Select Delete account and review the confirmation.

Request deletion without the app

Email us from the address associated with your Roofmates account. Include only the email address used for Roofmates and the words “Delete my Roofmates account.” Do not include a password, verification code, payment details, or identity document in the first message.

Email a deletion request

We will verify that you control the account through a safe account or provider verification step. Roofmates support will never ask you to send your password or an authentication code by email.

What is deleted

After verification and resolution of any unsettled obligations, Roofmates deactivates memberships, removes or de-identifies account-level profile and contact information, retires notification tokens, and requests deletion of the associated Firebase Authentication account.

What may be retained

Shared expense, settlement, dispute, security, and audit records that other participants rely on may remain under a non-identifying “Deleted user” label for ledger integrity, fraud prevention, dispute handling, legal compliance, and the rights of other users. Encrypted backups may retain deleted data until their normal backup cycle expires.

If deletion is temporarily blocked

Roofmates identifies unsettled obligations that must be resolved before deletion can complete. After they are settled, submit the request again. Contact support if you believe the block is incorrect.
